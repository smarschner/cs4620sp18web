CS 4621: Lecture 7

Textures

  • Texture is another means to pass information from Javascript to WebGL.
  • A texture is an image that you can plaster onto a geometric primitive to give it high-frequency varying surface details, not just the interpolated smooth stuffs you get from rasterization.
